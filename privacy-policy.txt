# Privacy Policy for Deutschway Chrome Extension

**Last Updated:** June 2026

## Introduction

Deutschway ("we", "our", or "us") provides a Chrome browser extension designed to help users learn German vocabulary by displaying daily words and opening the Deutschway website for learning activities.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally

The extension may store the following information locally within your browser:

* Daily learning progress
* Word completion status
* User preferences and settings
* Date of the last automatic website opening

This information is stored only on your device using Chrome's storage functionality.

### Information We Do Not Collect

The extension does **not** collect:

* Names
* Email addresses
* Passwords
* Financial information
* Government identification numbers
* Health information
* Personal messages
* Browsing history
* Website content viewed by users

## How the Extension Works

The extension:

1. Displays German vocabulary words to help users learn German.
2. Opens the Deutschway website once per day to provide learning content.
3. Stores learning progress locally in the browser.

## Website Data

When the extension opens the Deutschway website, standard web server logs may be collected by the website hosting provider, including:

* IP address
* Browser type
* Device information
* Date and time of access

This information is used for security, analytics, and website operation purposes.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except where required by law or necessary to operate the website and hosting services.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information and maintain the security of our services.

## Third-Party Services

The Deutschway website may use third-party services such as:

* Website hosting providers
* Analytics providers

These services may process technical information necessary for their operation.

## Your Choices

You can:

* Disable or remove the extension at any time through Chrome.
* Clear locally stored extension data through your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy

The extension is not specifically directed toward children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
